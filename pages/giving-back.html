Our Story/ Giving Back

Our Dedication to Giving

The Brilliant Earth Foundation aims to extend our positive impact not just to our supply chain and customer communities, but also in the greater communities where diamonds, gemstones, and precious metals are sourced. We contribute to programs focused on Responsible Sourcing, Social Impact, and Climate Action.

Empowering Miners through
Sustainable Practices

Brilliant Earth supports Pure Earth’s mission to empower miners in the artisanal gold mining community of Madre de Dios by providing training in mercury-free mining practices. Pure Earth will also work to restore rainforest areas that have been degraded by gold-mining practices while fostering sustainable methods of mine closures.

Learn More

Confronting U.S. Food Insecurity With
Feeding America

By donating to groups like Feeding America's COVID-19 Response Fund, we support hunger relief. Brilliant Earth is also matching employee donations to aid in Feeding America’s efforts to combat food insecurity in America and end US hunger.

Shop Diamond that Care

Fighting Deforestation in Partnership
With the Rainforest Alliance

Brilliant Earth thoughtfully designed the Rainforest Alliance Pendant with change in mind and donates to support their mission to create a more sustainable world in the Amazon and beyond. The Rainforest Alliance advocates for and advances best practices for land management.

Shop the Neckalce

Providing Eaducation through the
Brilliant Mobile School

Brilliant Earth partnered with the Diamond Development Initiative to fund a primary school in a rural diamond mining community, providing education to two classes of children who had little or no formal education and may otherwise accompany their parents to the nearby Lungudi mine. We have also supported these classes through High School.
