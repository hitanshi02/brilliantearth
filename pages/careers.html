Our Story / careers

Join the Team

View Open Positions

Brilliant Earth is one of the fastest growing ecommerce jewelers in the world, and the leader in ethical origin fine jewelry. We are on the lookout for bright, motivated candidates who are eager to make an impact from day one and grow with a team of passionate and kind people!

Mission Mindset

At Brilliant Earth, our mission to transform the jewelry industry is at the center of everything we do. As a member of the Brilliant Earth team you will be an empowered and educated advocate of the mission in your role and beyond.

Celebrate Success

At Brilliant Earth you will be celebrated for your successes, both big and small. We enjoy having frequent team happy hours, lunches, and all-company outings.

Make an Impact

From your first day at Brilliant Earth you will be able to work on meaningful projects and make a real impact on the company. You will be supported by your team and receive the support and mentorship to succeed through regular one on one check ins.

Volunteer

In addition to donating 5% of profits to communities impacted by the jewelry trade, we also are committed to giving back in our local communities. While working at Brilliant Earth, you will be given a variety of opportunities to volunteer, from office food drives to planting community gardens!
